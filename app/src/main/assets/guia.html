Game Setup Menu

Welcome to the game setup menu of our betting game. Here you can configure your game before starting to play:

Game Configuration:

In the "Username" field, enter your name so you can be identified in the game.

In the "Money" field, you can see the number of coins you have.

Remember, you have the option to change the language of the game.

Buttons:

  • The "Start Game" button will take you to the game menu with the entered configuration.
  • The "Turn On/Off Music" button will allow you to control the background music playback.
  • The "Select Music" button will allow you to choose the background melody for your game.

Game Menu

Welcome to the game menu of our betting game. Here you can play the game:

Game Interface:

Your username is displayed below the "Username" field.

Your remaining coins are displayed below the "Money" field.

Buttons:

  • The "Play" button will deduct one coin from your wallet and start the game. You will receive a notification with your winnings.
  • The "Turn On/Off Music" button will allow you to control the background music playback.
  • The "Select Music" button will allow you to choose the background melody for your game.
  • The "Exit and View Rankings" button will end the current game and take you to the rankings screen.

Ranking Menu

Welcome to the ranking menu of our betting game. Here you can view a list of all the games played in the game:

Ranking Information:

In the ranking list, you will see the following information for each game:

  • The player's name
  • The date of the game
  • The number of coins

Buttons:

  • The "Play Again" button allows you to start a new game.
  • The "Take Screenshot" button allows you to capture the ranking list as an image and save it to your device.
  • The "Save Score to Calendar" button allows you to save the ranking list and scores to your device's calendar.
  • The "Exit Game" button allows you to exit the game and close the application.

Enjoy viewing the rankings and have fun playing our game!
